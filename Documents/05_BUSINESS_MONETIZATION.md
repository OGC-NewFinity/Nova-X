# Business Monetization

## Revenue Models

### Freemium Model
- **Free Tier** - Basic theme generation with limitations
- **Premium Tier** - Advanced features and unlimited generation
- **Enterprise Tier** - Custom solutions and dedicated support

### Pricing Strategy
- Subscription-based licensing
- One-time premium purchase option
- Enterprise custom pricing
- Add-on marketplace

## Target Customer Segments
1. **Individual Users** - Personal projects and portfolios
2. **Agencies** - Client theme development
3. **Enterprises** - Large-scale WordPress deployments
4. **Developers** - Professional theme creation tools

## Revenue Streams
- Premium subscriptions
- Enterprise licenses
- Marketplace commissions
- Professional services
- Training and certification
